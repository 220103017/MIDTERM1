Volunteering Experience

ZhasUlan and Astana Zhastary Volunteering Organizations

For three years, I have been an active volunteer with ZhasUlan and Astana Zhastary, two of Kazakhstan's leading volunteer organizations. Through these organizations, I've had the opportunity to contribute to various community projects, including helping families in need and supporting sick children. These experiences have taught me the importance of giving back to the community and making a difference in people's lives.

Concerts and Events

I've also had the unique opportunity to volunteer at concerts by well-known artists such as Dimash Kudaibergen, Egor Krid, and other celebrities. Being part of these large-scale events allowed me to improve my organizational and teamwork skills while ensuring the smooth execution of the concerts.

Environmental Efforts

Every Sunday, I take part in cleaning the natural areas around Astana, helping to preserve the beauty of our local environment. This is a personal initiative that I am passionate about, as it helps maintain the cleanliness and sustainability of the city's natural spaces.
