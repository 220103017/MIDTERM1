My Hobbies

Playing Musical Instruments

Music has always been a big part of my life. I enjoy playing various musical instruments, particularly:
Dombyra: A traditional Kazakh instrument, which I love to play for its deep cultural roots.
Piano: I appreciate the versatility of the piano and enjoy playing both classical and modern pieces.
Zhetigen: Another traditional Kazakh instrument that allows me to connect with the heritage of my country.

Camping in the Mountains

I love spending time outdoors, especially camping in the mountains. Being in nature helps me disconnect from the busy world, and I enjoy hiking, setting up a tent, and taking in the beautiful landscapes of Kazakhstan.

Reading Kazakh Literature

I am passionate about Kazakh literature, which offers a deep insight into our culture and history. Some of my favorite authors include Abay Kunanbayev and Mukhtar Auezov. Reading these works allows me to reflect on the richness of the Kazakh language and heritage.

Pictures by Numbers

I enjoy relaxing by working on Pictures by Numbers. This hobby allows me to express my creativity while staying focused. It’s a great way to unwind and create beautiful pieces of art with simple, guided steps.
